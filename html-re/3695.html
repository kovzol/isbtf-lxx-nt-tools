Peshitta (3695)
Peshitta: Text ܡܶܢ ܠܥܶܠ ܐܶܡܰܪ ܕ݁ܕ݂ܶܒ݂ܚܶܐ ܘܩܽܘܪܒ݁ܳܢܶܐ ܘܝܰܩܕ݁ܶܐ ܫܰܠܡܶܐ ܕ݁ܰܚܠܳܦ݂ ܚܛܳܗܶܐ ܠܳܐ ܨܒ݂ܰܝܬ݁ ܗܳܢܽܘܢ ܕ݁ܡܶܬ݂ܩܰܪܒ݂ܺܝܢ ܗ݈ܘܰܘ ܒ݁ܢܳܡܽܘܣܳܐ܂

Peshitta: engl. Übersetzung After saying above, Sacrifices and offerings and whole burnt offerings in place of sacrifices for sin thou hast not taken pleasure, which are offered according to the law,
