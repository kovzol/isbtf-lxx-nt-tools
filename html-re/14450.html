Peshitta (14450)
Peshitta: Text ܐܳܡܰܪ ܠܗܽܘܢ ܦ݁ܰܘܠܳܘܣ ܠܳܐ ܝܳܕ݂ܰܥ ܗ݈ܘܺܝܬ݂ ܐܰܚܰܝ ܕ݁ܟ݂ܳܗܢܳܐ ܗ݈ܘ ܟ݁ܬ݂ܺܝܒ݂ ܗ݈ܽܘ ܓ݁ܶܝܪ ܕ݁ܰܠܪܺܫܳܐ ܕ݁ܥܰܡܳܟ݂ ܠܳܐ ܬ݁ܠܽܘܛ܂

Peshitta: engl. Übersetzung Paul said to them, I was not aware, my brethren, that he was high priest; for it is written, You shall not curse a ruler of your people.
