Peshitta (4693)
Peshitta: Text ܐܳܡܰܪ ܠܶܗ ܐܰܝܠܶܝܢ ܗܽܘ ܕ݁ܶܝܢ ܝܶܫܽܘܥ ܐܶܡܰܪ ܠܶܗ ܕ݁ܠܳܐ ܬ݁ܶܩܛܽܘܠ ܘܠܳܐ ܬ݁ܓ݂ܽܘܪ ܘܠܳܐ ܬ݁ܶܓ݂ܢܽܘܒ݂ ܘܠܳܐ ܬ݁ܶܣܗܰܕ݂ ܣܳܗܕ݁ܽܘܬ݂ ܫܽܘܩܪܳܐ܂

Peshitta: engl. Übersetzung He said to him, Which ones? And Jesus said to him, you shall not commit murder and you shall not commit adultery and you shall not steal and you shall not bear false witness;
