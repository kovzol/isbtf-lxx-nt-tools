Peshitta (7481)
Peshitta: Text ܐܶܠܳܐ ܐܰܝܟ݂ ܕ݁ܰܟ݂ܬ݂ܺܝܒ݂ ܕ݁ܥܰܝܢܳܐ ܠܳܐ ܚܙܳܬ݂ ܘܶܐܕ݂ܢܳܐ ܠܳܐ ܫܶܡܥܰܬ݂ ܘܥܰܠ ܠܶܒ݁ܳܐ ܕ݁ܒ݂ܰܪܢܳܫܳܐ ܠܳܐ ܣܠܶܩ ܡܶܕ݁ܶܡ ܕ݁ܛܰܝܶܒ݂ ܐܰܠܳܗܳܐ ܠܰܐܝܠܶܝܢ ܕ݁ܪܳܚܡܺܝܢ ܠܶܗ܂

Peshitta: engl. Übersetzung but just as it is written, Things which eye has not seen and ear has not heard, and which have not entered the heart of man, all that God has prepared for those who love him.
