Peshitta (7401)
Peshitta: Text ܟ݁ܺܐܢܽܘܬ݂ܳܐ ܕ݁ܶܝܢ ܕ݁ܰܒ݂ܗܰܝܡܳܢܽܘܬ݂ܳܐ ܗܳܟ݂ܰܢܳܐ ܐܳܡܪܳܐ ܕ݁ܠܳܐ ܬ݁ܺܐܡܰܪ ܒ݁ܠܶܒ݁ܳܟ݂ ܕ݁ܡܰܢܽܘ ܣܠܶܩ ܠܰܫܡܰܝܳܐ ܘܰܐܚܶܬ݂ ܠܰܡܫܺܝܚܳܐ܂

Peshitta: engl. Übersetzung But the righteousness based on faith speaks thus, Do not say in your heart, Who will ascend into heaven and bring Christ down?
