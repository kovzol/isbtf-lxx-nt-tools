Peshitta (3691)
Peshitta: Text ܡܶܛܽܠ ܗܳܢܳܐ ܟ݁ܰܕ݂ ܥܳܐܶܠ ܠܥܳܠܡܳܐ ܐܶܡܰܪ ܒ݁ܕ݂ܶܒ݂ܚܶܐ ܘܩܽܘܪܒ݁ܳܢܶܐ ܠܳܐ ܨܒ݂ܰܝܬ݁ ܦ݁ܰܓ݂ܪܳܐ ܕ݁ܶܝܢ ܐܰܠܒ݁ܶܫܬ݁ܳܢܝ܂

Peshitta: engl. Übersetzung Therefore, when he comes into the world, he says, In sacrifices and offerings thou hast not taken pleasure, but a body thou hast prepared for me;
